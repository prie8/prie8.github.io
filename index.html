hi, i'm priya

I'm a researcher interested in the intersection of synthetic biology and computation. Welcome to my portfolio.

It's a collection of my projects, research questions, and personal reflections. Here I explore how we can engineer biological systems to solve complex health challenges, while also documenting my journey in leadership and community service.

I believe that the best science often happens at the boundaries of disciplines. Whether I'm coding models for cell behavior or experimenting with wild yeast fermentation in my kitchen, I'm always looking for underlying patterns and new ways to understand the living world.

I'm currently focused on agent-based modeling of microbial communities and their applications in personalized medicine. My research has been recognized at ISEF (Finalist) and AAAS (Fellow), exploring everything from diabetic wound healing to Long COVID mechanisms.
